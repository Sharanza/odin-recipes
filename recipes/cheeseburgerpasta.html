Cheeseburger Pasta Recipe

Description

One day I will make a good cheeseburger pasta.

Ingredients

  • 500g Lean Beef Mince
  • 400g Dried Pasta
  • 1 Whole Chopped Onion
  • 2tsp Smoked Paprika
  • ½tsp Onion Granules
  • ½tsp Garlic Granules
  • ½tsp Mustard Powder
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 1tbsp tomato puree
  • 1tbsp Worcester sauce
  • 900ml Boiling water & beef stock
  • 120g Grated Cheese

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and put in a pot with the beef mince on a high heat until browned.
  2. Add the tomato puree and worcester sauce, mix in and then add the remaining spices.
  3. Stir in until mixed well then add the dry pasta and coat in the mixture.
  4. Add the stock, turn the heat up to a boil then leave to simmer until the pasta has cooked and the water has soaked up, then add the cheese and give a mix.
